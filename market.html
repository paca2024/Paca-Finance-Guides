Click on BUY STAKE to purchase available stakes.

4. Enter Listing Number:

Input the Listing Number into box (example: 2)

5. Confirm Understanding:

Click on the tick box next to "I understand and agree" information.

6. Buy Stake:

Click on BUY STAKE to buy stake. This will require two wallet transactions:

  1. First transaction: Approve the purchase
  2. Second transaction: Complete the stake purchase

Both transactions will need to be confirmed in your wallet.

4. How to Sell Stakes:

Follow these steps to sell your stakes:

Step 1: Find Available Stakes

Navigate to the marketplace to view available stakes.

Step 2: Click SELL STAKE

Find and click the SELL STAKE button.

Step 3: Enter Stake ID

Enter your stake ID in the provided field.

Step 4: Set Price and Discount

Choose your selling price and set any discount.

Step 5: Complete the Sale

Before finalizing your listing:

  • Review your stake details and price
  • Tick the checkbox to confirm you understand the terms
  • Click confirm to list your stake
  • Confirm the transaction in your wallet

After confirmation, you should now see your listed stakes in My Listed Stakes.

Updating Sale Price:

  1. Input the stake ID you want to adjust in the box (example: 44)
  2. Enter the new price
  3. Click on UPDATE SALE PRICE button

Cancel Listing:

To remove your stake from the marketplace:

  1. Add the stake ID number in the box
  2. Tick the checkbox to confirm you understand
  3. Click on CANCEL SALE
  4. Accept the transaction in your wallet
