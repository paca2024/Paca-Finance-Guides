Marketplace Guide

1. Connect Your Wallet

Important: For BSC chain transactions, ensure you have:
  • BNB in your wallet for gas fees
  • USDT for purchases

2. Accessing the Marketplace

Click on MARKETPLACE in the navigation menu to access the trading interface.

4. How to Sell Stakes

1. Listing your stakes:

Scroll down to view your available stakes that you want to sell.

Click on SELL STAKE to begin listing your stake for sale.

2. Enter Stake ID:

Add the stake ID number in the box for the stake you want to sell (example: 44)

3. Set Your Price:

Input the price you would like to sell your stake for.

Choose a discount percentage to help sell your stake faster:

4. Complete the Sale:

Before finalizing your listing:

  • Review your stake details and price
  • Tick the checkbox to confirm you understand the terms
  • Click confirm to list your stake
