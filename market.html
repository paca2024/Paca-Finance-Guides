Market Guide

How to Buy Stakes

1. Connect Your Wallet

Ensure your wallet is connected to the Paca Finance platform.

2. Navigate to Market

Go to the Market section in the platform navigation.

3. Browse Available Stakes

View the list of available stakes for purchase. Each listing shows:

  • Stake amount
  • Time remaining
  • Current price
  • Expected returns
4. Purchase Stake

Click "Buy" on your chosen stake and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Important: Always verify the stake details and price before confirming your purchase.

How to Sell Stakes

1. Access Your Stakes

Go to "My Stakes" in your dashboard.

2. Select Stake to Sell

Choose the stake you want to sell and click "List for Sale".

3. Set Your Price

Enter your desired selling price. Consider:

  • Current market value
  • Time remaining on stake
  • Accumulated rewards
4. Confirm Listing

Review the details and confirm to list your stake on the market.

Tip: Monitor market prices regularly to ensure your listing remains competitive.
