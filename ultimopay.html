How to Use UltimoPay

Dashboard Overview

The UltimoPay dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your crypto assets and available actions:

US Dollar Balance

View your USD balance and manage fiat transactions

  • Track your USD balance
  • Load funds to your card
  • Monitor exchange rates

Card Management

Manage your UltimoPay card directly from the dashboard

  • View card balance
  • Load/recharge card
  • Track transactions

How to Use UltimoPay Features

Depositing Funds

  1. Select your desired cryptocurrency from the dashboard
  2. Click the "Deposit" button
  3. Follow the provided wallet address or QR code instructions
  4. Wait for blockchain confirmation

Loading Your Card

  1. Navigate to the Card section
  2. View your current card balance
  3. To load card you will need to sell crypto to USD, click on the sell button on the crypto you want to sell
  4. Enter the amount you want to sell and click on sell
  5. Go back to dashboard and you should now see your funds in US dollar
  6. Click on load(charge), it will ask you to input the amount you want to load your card, (min $100) this will come from your US Dollar amount then click on load card

Withdrawing Funds

  1. Choose the cryptocurrency you want to withdraw
  2. Click "Withdraw"
  3. Enter the recipient's wallet address
  4. Confirm the amount and transaction details
  5. Complete any required security verifications

Trading Crypto currencies

  1. Select the cryptocurrency you want to trade
  2. Choose "Buy" or "Sell" based on your needs
  3. Enter the amount you want to trade
  4. Review the exchange rate and fees
  5. Confirm the transaction
