THE VAULT

Key Features

• You do not have to be in the pools to be in the vault.
• Vault stakes do not compound, they are static.
• Vault Bonuses are in Stables.
• Each Tier has to be on the same Chain.

Vesting Period & Unlocking

Stakes in the vaults will go in on a vesting period of 250 days but every 25 days, 10% of your assets are unlocked so you can:

  • Re-stake
  • Sell
  • Leave them in to maintain your tier

Bonus Structure

Whatever the dollar amount at the time you staked the asset, you will get a 10% bonus.

Example Calculation

For a $3,500 tier 1 deposit:

• Every 25 days: 10% of stake = $350
• Bonus payment: 10% of $350 = $35
• Total per period: $385

This will also increase your new pool stakes to 0.34% daily (0.41% during promos)

Important Information

  • The bonus Tier for Vault deposits will count for any stakes made after you hit the tier.
  • It won't count for stakes already registered.
  • Each Vault is chain specific - you can hit a separate Tier on each chain.
  • The bonus % applies to the stable coin Pool on that specific chain.

Automatic APR Boost

When you make a new stake in the Pools tab, the system automatically checks your Vaults to see if you qualify for the APR Boost and applies it if eligible.

⚠️ Important Note

If you claim your Tokens and your Investment Total goes down, you may drop out of your Tier. To maintain your tier, simply top it up with a coin/Token on the same chain.
