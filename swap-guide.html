How to Swap BEP20 USDC to Sonic USDCe

Step 1: Access SwapX

Click on the button below to access SwapX using the Paca Finance referral link:

Open SwapX with Referral

Step 2: Navigate to Bridge

Locate and click on the "Bridge" option in the interface

Step 3: Connect Your Wallet

Click on "Connect Wallet" and select your wallet provider

Ensure your wallet is set to the correct network before proceeding

Step 4: Enter Amount

Input the amount of BEP20 USDC you want to swap to Sonic USDCe

Step 5: Approve Transaction

Click "Approve" in your wallet to authorize the transaction

Always verify the transaction details in your wallet before approving

Step 6: Confirmation

Once completed, you should see a confirmation of your successful swap
