How to Create a Stake with a Mobile Wallet

This guide will help you create and manage stakes using popular mobile wallets such as Trust Wallet, MetaMask Mobile, and others.

Step 1: Connect Your Mobile Wallet

First, ensure your mobile wallet is properly set up and connected to the BSC network.

Step 2: Access Paca Finance

Open your mobile wallet's built-in browser and navigate to the Paca Finance website.

Step 3: Select Your Wallet

Click on the three bars (‚ò∞) next to the PACA name and click on "Select Wallet" to choose your preferred wallet connection method.

Step 4: Verify Connection

Once connected, you'll see "Disconnect" in the top right corner of the screen, indicating your wallet is successfully linked to Paca Finance.

Step 5: How to Create a Stake

To create your stake:

  1. Click on "Create Stake"
  2. Enter the amount you wish to stake
  3. Make sure to use whole numbers with a decimal

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Always include the decimal point and zeros (e.g., 100.00, not 100)

Step 6: Approve and Create Stake

Before creating your stake, you must:

  1. Check the "I acknowledge" box
  2. Click "Approve" and confirm in your wallet
  3. Once approved, click "Create Stake"

‚ö†Ô∏è Note: The approval step is required only for your first stake. Subsequent stakes will only need the acknowledgment and create stake steps.

Step 7: Verify Your Stake

To verify your stake has been created:

  1. Refresh the page
  2. You should now see your stake displayed
