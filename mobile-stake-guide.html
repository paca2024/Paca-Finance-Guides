How to Create a Stake with a Mobile Wallet

üì± Mobile Wallet Support

This guide will help you create and manage stakes using popular mobile wallets such as Trust Wallet, MetaMask Mobile, and others.

Step 1: Connect Your Mobile Wallet

First, ensure your mobile wallet is properly set up and connected to the BSC network.

Step 2: Access Paca Finance

Open your mobile wallet's built-in browser and navigate to the Paca Finance website.

Step 3: Navigate to Staking

Click on the "Staking" section to view available staking options.

Step 4: Choose Stake Amount

Select the amount you wish to stake and review the terms.

Step 5: Confirm Transaction

Review and confirm the transaction in your mobile wallet.

üí° Mobile Staking Tips

  • Always ensure you have enough BNB for gas fees
  • Double-check all transaction details before confirming
  • Keep your wallet app updated to the latest version
  • Never share your wallet's seed phrase with anyone
