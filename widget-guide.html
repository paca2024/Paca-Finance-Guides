How to Use the Crypto Exchange Widget

Step 1: Select Source Coin

Click on the coin you want to exchange (you send)

Step 2: Select Target Coin

Find and select the coin you want to get (you get)

Step 3: Initiate Exchange

Click on the "Exchange" button to start the process

Step 4: Enter Recipient Wallet

You'll be asked to choose where you want the funds sent to:

  • Choose your preferred wallet (e.g., Rabby.io or MetaMask)
  • Paste your wallet address into the "Recipient Wallet" field

Step 5: Confirm Exchange

Click on the "Exchange" button to proceed with the transaction

Step 6: Send Funds

The system will provide you with an address to send your funds to

Important:

Make sure to send exactly the amount requested to the provided address

Step 7: Completion

You will see the crypto you have exchanged to in your wallet

Important Reminder:

Please send only what it asks for and the correct amount. Sending incorrect amounts may result in failed transactions.
