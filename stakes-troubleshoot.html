What to Do If Your Stakes Don't Show on the UI

Important Note: If your stakes are not displaying correctly in the interface, don't worry! Your stakes are safely stored on the blockchain. This guide will help you troubleshoot and resolve display issues.

1Verify Your Wallet Connection

First, ensure your wallet is properly connected to Paca Finance:

  • Check if your wallet is connected to the correct network (BSC, BASE, or SONIC)
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting your wallet
  • Clear your browser cache and refresh the page

2Check Your Network

Make sure you're on the correct network:

  1. Open your wallet
  2. Check the current network
  3. Switch to the appropriate network:
    • Binance Smart Chain (BSC)
    • Base Network (BASE)
    • SONIC Chain
  4. Refresh the Paca Finance page

3Verify Your Stakes on Block Explorer

You can always verify your stakes directly on the blockchain using the appropriate block explorer:

Block Explorers by Network:

  • BSC: BSCScan
  • BASE: BaseScan
  • SONIC: SONIC Explorer

To check your stakes:

  1. Copy your wallet address
  2. Visit the appropriate block explorer for your network
  3. Paste your wallet address in the search bar
  4. Check the "Internal Transactions" tab

Note: Your stakes will always be visible on the blockchain explorer, as they are permanently recorded on the blockchain.

4Common Solutions

Try these quick fixes:

  • Hard refresh your browser (Ctrl + F5)
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try a different browser
  • Disable and re-enable any wallet extensions

5Contact Admin Team in TG

If you've tried all the above steps and still can't see your stakes:

Before contacting the admin team, please have ready:

  • Your wallet address
  • Transaction hash of your stake
  • Screenshots of the issue
  • List of troubleshooting steps you've already tried

Admin Team is ready to help resolve any issues you're experiencing.
