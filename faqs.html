Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Paca Finance?
Paca Finance is a decentralized finance (DeFi) platform that offers various financial services including staking, vaults, and token swaps. Our platform is designed to provide users with secure and efficient ways to manage and grow their digital assets.
How do I get started with Paca Finance?
To get started:
  1. Connect your Web3 wallet (like MetaMask)
  2. Ensure you have the required tokens
  3. Choose your preferred investment option (Staking or Vault)
  4. Follow the guide specific to your chosen option

Staking Questions

What are the benefits of staking?
Staking offers several benefits:
  • Earn Daily Funds
  • Participate in platform governance
  • Support network security
  • Access to exclusive features
How long is the staking period?
Staking periods can vary based on your chosen tier and preferences. Check the specific staking guide for detailed information about available periods and their respective rewards.
When can I compound or claim my daily funds?
You have two options when your daily funds accumulate:
  • Compound: When your daily funds reach $20, you can compound it back into your stake - this will make a new stake
  • Claim: Wait until your daily funds reach $25, and you can claim them back to your wallet
Are the funds Locked?
Yes, your initial deposit is locked for 250 days. Once 250 days have expired, you can withdraw or restake.
What is the minimum value you can stake?
Minimum stake is $15.00

Vault Questions

How do vaults work?
Vault deposit assets are not directly used in the Concentrated Liquidity Pools that the team farms to generate external revenue. This is because there is no way to sustainably use the actual growth assets to farm and still give investors back the full value of their assets, if those assets were to increase in price ‚Äî Which, of course, is the whole point of owning them.

Instead, our strategy to cover the APR Boost and Bonus works as follows:
  • We have an official partnership with a major lending firm (to be announced soon)
  • A portion of our back-end treasury assets, along with a small percentage of vault deposits, is leveraged with our lending partner
  • The funds borrowed are then farmed to generate additional yield
  • This yield offsets the Boost and Bonus rewards, while ensuring the dollar value of users vault assets reflect the live market value
  • The borrowed funds are repaid with CLP profits, and any necessary rewards are distributed to the vaults as needed

This approach is simple, low-risk, and ensures sustainability. While it doesn't generate much in the way of direct profit for the team, it provides a reliable way for the community to earn safely while making our platform more attractive in the long run.
What are the vault tiers?
Vaults offer different tiers with varying benefits and requirements. Each tier has its own minimum deposit requirement and reward structure. Refer to the vault guide for specific details about each tier.
What is The Warchest?
The Warchest serves as a distinct treasury for the Developers at PACA Finance. This fund is used to manage any airdrops, extra profits, or additional revenue derived from their own investments.

Having a treasury is crucial; any extra income generated will be directed toward ensuring you continue to earn rewards on your investment, rather than being used for promotions or giveaways.

Think of it as a rainy-day fund ‚Äî an essential component for sound investment strategies and long-term sustainability.
