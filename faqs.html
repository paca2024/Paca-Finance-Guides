Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Paca Finance?
Paca Finance is a decentralized finance (DeFi) platform that offers various financial services including staking, vaults, and token swaps. Our platform is designed to provide users with secure and efficient ways to manage and grow their digital assets.
How do I get started with Paca Finance?
To get started:
  1. Connect your Web3 wallet (like MetaMask)
  2. Ensure you have the required tokens
  3. Choose your preferred investment option (Staking or Vault)
  4. Follow the guide specific to your chosen option

Staking Questions

What are the benefits of staking?
Staking offers several benefits:
  • Earn Daily Funds
  • Participate in platform governance
  • Support network security
  • Access to exclusive features
What is the minimum value you can stake?
Minimum stake is $15.00
How long is the staking period?
Staking periods can vary based on your chosen tier and preferences. Check the specific staking guide for detailed information about available periods and their respective rewards.

Vault Questions

How do vaults work?
Vaults are automated yield-generating strategies that:
  • Pool user funds together
  • Generate optimal returns
  • Distribute rewards to participants
  • Operate with smart contract security
What are the vault tiers?
Vaults offer different tiers with varying benefits and requirements. Each tier has its own minimum deposit requirement and reward structure. Refer to the vault guide for specific details about each tier.
