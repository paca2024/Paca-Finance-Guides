QR Code Guide for Mobile Users

How to Use QR Codes

QR codes provide a quick and easy way to access Paca Finance on your mobile device. Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Open Your Mobile Wallet

Launch your preferred mobile wallet app (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.)

Step 2: Access the QR Scanner

Look for the scan or QR code icon in your wallet app

Step 3: Scan the QR Code

Point your camera at the QR code displayed on the Paca Finance website

Step 4: Confirm Connection

Your wallet will prompt you to confirm the connection to Paca Finance
