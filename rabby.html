How to Create a New Rabby Wallet on Mobile

1. Download Rabby Wallet

First, download the Rabby Wallet app from your device's app store:

  • For iOS: Download from the App Store
  • For Android: Download from the Google Play Store

2. Create New Wallet

After installing the app:

  1. Open Rabby Wallet
  2. Click on "Create New Wallet"
  3. Follow the security setup process

3. Secure Your Recovery Phrase

Important:
  • Write down your recovery phrase in the exact order
  • Store it in a safe place
  • Never share it with anyone
  • Rabby support will never ask for your recovery phrase

4. Set Up Security

Protect your wallet:

  1. Create a strong password
  2. Enable biometric authentication if available
  3. Set up additional security features

5. Add Networks

Configure your wallet for BSC:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Select "Networks"
  3. Enable Binance Smart Chain (BSC)

6. Ready to Use

Your Rabby Wallet is now ready for:

  • Connecting to Paca Finance
  • Managing your assets
  • Making transactions
