Gas Fee Optimization Guide

Important Note: This guide will help you reduce transaction costs by optimizing gas fees when using MetaMask wallet with Paca Finance.

1Use a Different Network

BSC (Binance Smart Chain) has much lower gas fees than Ethereum. Since you're using BSC testnet, make sure you're also using BSC mainnet for real transactions.

2Manually Adjust Gas Fees in MetaMask

  1. When confirming a transaction, click "Edit" next to "Gas Fee"
  2. Choose "Advanced Options"
  3. Set a lower gas price (GWEI) based on current network conditions
  4. Confirm the transaction (it may take longer, but costs less)

3Use a Gas Fee Tracker

Check real-time gas fees before sending transactions:

  • BSC: https://bscscan.com/gastracker

4Avoid Peak Hours

Important: Gas fees are higher when the network is busy.

Try late at night or early morning (UTC time) for lower fees.

5Use a Gas Fee Optimization Service

Some wallets and dApps help reduce gas fees automatically:

  • Rabby Wallet
  • 1inch
  • Balancer

6Enable MetaMask "Enhanced Gas Fee UI"

  1. Open MetaMask Settings
  2. Go to Experimental Features
  3. Enable Enhanced Gas Fee UI to get better estimates
