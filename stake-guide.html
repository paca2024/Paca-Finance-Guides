My Stake Has Hit 0 Days - What Next?

Step 1: Enter Your Stake ID

Start by entering your stake ID in the input field.

Step 2: Make Your Decision

Decide what action to take:

  • Re-stake your funds
  • Withdraw all funds
  • Combination of both

Step 3: For Re-staking

Simply click on "Confirm" and action the transaction in your wallet.

Step 4: For Full Withdrawal

Enter "0" in the box and click "Confirm"

Important: 36-Hour Wait Period

After confirming, you must wait 36 hours. This delay allows the team to calculate total payments and prepare farm withdrawals.

Step 5: After 36 Hours

  1. Navigate to the Paca site
  2. Click on the withdrawal icon
  3. Enter your Stake ID (e.g., 219)
  4. Click "Withdraw"
  5. Click "Begin signing process"

Step 6: Check Withdrawal Timer

To check how long you need to wait before you can claim your withdrawal:

  1. Click on the BSC symbol
  2. This will show you the countdown timer for when your stake will be available for withdrawal

Keep track of this timer to know exactly when you can withdraw your stake

Note:

If you receive an error message, it likely means the 36-hour waiting period has not yet elapsed. Please wait the full duration and try again.
